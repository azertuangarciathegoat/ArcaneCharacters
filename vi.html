VI

The Piltover Enforcer

Vi is a tough, no-nonsense enforcer from the streets of Zaun who now works alongside Caitlyn in Piltover. With her powerful hextech gauntlets, she fights for justice while navigating the complex relationship between the two cities.

Equipped with her pink hextech gauntlets, Vi is a force of nature in combat. She's fiercely loyal to her friends and will do whatever it takes to protect Zaun and Piltover from those who would do harm.

About Vi

Biography

Vi was once a street kid in Zaun, learning to fight to survive. After being taken in by Professor Heimerdinger, she became a key member of the Piltover Police Force. Her tough exterior hides a loyal heart.

Abilities

  • Hextech Gauntlets - Powerful pink energy-fist weapons
  • Dockage - Massive area knockback
  • Flash - Forward dash with power

Allies & Enemies

Vi works alongside Caitlyn as her partner. Her complicated past with Jinx (who she once knew as Powder) drives her to protect the innocent.

Quotes
