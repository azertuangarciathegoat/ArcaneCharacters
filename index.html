CAITLYN

The Sheriff of Piltover

Caitlyn is Piltover's finest sharpshooter and sheriff. Known as the Sheriff of Piltover, she is a calm, calculated law enforcement leader who upholds justice in the City of Progress. With her signature rifle and hextech abilities, she works to maintain peace between Piltover and Zaun.

Armed with her trusted hextech rifle, Caitlyn is an expert marksman who never misses her target. She's the leader of the Piltover Police Force and one of the most skilled negotiators in the region.

About Caitlyn

Biography

Caitlyn is the Sheriff of Piltover and the leader of the city's police force. Known for her precision and integrity, she works tirelessly to maintain peace in the City of Progress.

Abilities

  • Hextech Rifle - Her signature weapon with precise targeting
  • Yordle Snap Trap - Deploys traps to immobilize enemies
  • Piltover Peacemaker - Powerful shot that penetrates barriers

Allies & Enemies

Partnered with Vi, Caitlyn works to bring justice to both Piltover and Zaun. Her main adversary is Jinx, the chaotic menace from Zaun.

Quotes

"The good of the many outweighs the good of the few."
