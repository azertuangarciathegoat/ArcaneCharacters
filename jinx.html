JINX

The Loose Cannon

Jinx is a chaotic yet gifted Zaunite explosives expert who lives for mayhem and disorder. Once known as Powder, she struggled to find her place in the world, until her talents for destruction made her the most wanted rebel in Zaun's history.

Armed with an arsenal of deadly weapons including her iconic Fishbones rocket launcher and Pow-Pow minigun, Jinx brings chaos wherever she goes. Her manic energy and unpredictable nature make her a force to be reckoned with in the underground city.

About Jinx

Biography

Once known as Powder, Jinx was a young Zaunite who suffered from trauma and mental instability. Her journey from a desperate orphan to the most dangerous criminal in Zaun is a tragic tale of loss and chaos.

Weapons

  • Fishbones - Rocket launcher with personality
  • Pow-Pow - Rapid-fire minigun
  • Zapper - Electric shock weapon

Allies & Enemies

Jinx's main rival is Caitlyn, the Sheriff of Piltover. Her complicated past with Vi adds depth to her chaotic present.

Quotes
